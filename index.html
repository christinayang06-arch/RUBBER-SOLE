In-House Mold + 3D Validation

Why it matters for brands

  • Validate structure earlier to reduce tooling rework and schedule risk.
  • Check thickness distribution and key stress areas before final tooling.
  • Improve 2C/3C boundary stability and bonding design.
  • Reduce cost from late changes during trial and mass production.

What we review before tooling

Gate / Flow logic
Shrinkage tolerance
Color boundary design
Flex risk points
